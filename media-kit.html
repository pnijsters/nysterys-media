How We Work

Content Style & Philosophy

Everything we create starts from the same place: keeping it real. Our audience keeps coming back because what they see is genuinely us. No filters, no scripts, no selling.

Our videos are rooted in everyday life. Fashion moments, getting ready, days out, things that made us laugh. Our followers chose to be here because it feels like the real thing, and we work hard to keep it that way.

Collaborations

When we partner with a brand, it becomes part of the content naturally. Something we wear, use or genuinely enjoy rather than something we announce. That is the only way it works for our audience.

Creative Freedom

We stay away from scripted content and rigid creative briefs. Our followers can tell immediately when something feels off, and that shows in the numbers. The brands we have worked with consistently see the best results when they give us the creative freedom to do it our way.

The Process

How We Work Together

01
Brief

You share your campaign brief and objectives with us. We review the fit and confirm the partnership.

02
Draft

We create the content and send you a draft for review before anything goes live.

03
Revisions

We accommodate up to two rounds of revisions to make sure you are happy with the direction.

04
Payment & Post

Payment is expected before or upon posting. Once confirmed, the content goes live and we share the post URL with you.

05
Results

After seven days we can share full post performance data including views, likes, shares, and reach.

Payment: We accept standard digital payment methods. Payment is due before or upon posting. We do not hold content for unpaid invoices.   Content: All posted content remains live indefinitely. Content is only removed in the event of non-payment or a breach of agreed terms.

Turnaround

We deliver a first draft within 48 to 96 hours of receiving a complete brief. The brand has 48 hours to review and respond. If no feedback is received within that window, the draft is considered approved and we will proceed to posting.

Disclosure

All paid and sponsored content is disclosed in accordance with FTC guidelines. Posts will be clearly labelled as paid partnerships or sponsored content.

Usage Rights

All content created remains the intellectual property of the creator. Brands do not receive rights to repost, repurpose, or redistribute the content on any channel without a separate written agreement and applicable fee. Exclusivity arrangements are available and priced on a case by case basis.
